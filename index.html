Chinese Communist Party: Mao Zedong

Founder of Chinese Communist Party and People's Republic of China

Officially known as the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) is the founding and governing political party of modern China. Since Mao Zedong established the People's Republic in 1949, the CCP has maintained a political monopoly and has presided over the nation's rapid economic development and climb to global dominance.
